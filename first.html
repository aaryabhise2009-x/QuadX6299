WHAT IS FIRST?

QuadX 6299 competes in the FIRST Tech Challenge, which is one of 3 programs offered by FIRST

FIRST

For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology

FIRST is a global youth organization that runs robot and STEM competitions for students of all ages. Their programs are designed to combine the excitement of sport with the rigor of science and engineering, giving students hands-on experience in design, programming, and teamwork.
