WHAT IS FIRST?

QuadX 6299 competes in the FIRST Tech Challenge, which is one of 3 programs offered by FIRST

FIRST

For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology

FIRST is a global youth organization that runs robot and STEM competitions for students of all ages. Their programs are designed to combine the excitement of sport with the rigor of science and engineering, giving students hands-on experience in design, programming, and teamwork.

FIRST PROGRAMS

Grades K – 8

FIRST LEGO LEAGUE (FLL)

Discover · Explore · Challenge

FLL introduces STEM concepts to younger students through three sub-programs: Discover (PreK–1), Explore (grades 2–4), and Challenge (grades 4–8). Each uses LEGO robotics to make science and engineering engaging and accessible, while teaching collaboration and creative problem-solving.

Visit FLL Website
Grades 7 – 12

FIRST TECH CHALLENGE (FTC)

FTC allows students in grades 7–12 to design, build, and program a robot over the course of the school year to compete in a game that changes every season. It develops real-world skills in mechanical engineering, software development, project management, and teamwork.

Visit FTC Website
Grades 9 – 12

FIRST ROBOTICS COMPETITION (FRC)

FRC is the flagship high school robotics competition. Teams of students build full-size robots over a compressed six-week build season, then compete at regional and national events. The scale, complexity, and professional mentorship make FRC the most advanced program FIRST offers.
