About the Event

Where industry meets
the next generation

Momentum is a free STEM industry expo hosted by QuadX Robotics, bringing local companies and startups face-to-face with middle and high school students.

01

Company Booths

Local STEM companies set up interactive booths showcasing what they do, the problems they solve, and the careers they offer. Each booth features a short 3–4 minute pitch followed by open Q&A.

02

Student Exploration

Students explore booths at their own pace using a STEM passport. They will collect stamps from each company they visit. Students get to connect with industry professionals in a quick and easy way.

03

Live Robot Demo

QuadX will have their competition robot on display throughout the event, with live demonstrations showing how FTC robotics works and how students can get involved in FIRST programs.

04

Real Connections

A talent interest board lets students leave their info for companies they loved. A mentor and sponsor table lets adults in the room invest in the next wave of STEM talent directly.

We are
QuadX

We are Viperbots Team QuadX 6299 from Vandegrift High School in Austin Texas. We compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge and create robots yearly. We are a student-led team that strives to push the boundaries of innovation and learn as much as possible. We also do outreach to interact with the engineering community. This involves participating in company tours and hosting our own events to teach the younger generation.

Momentum is an outreach event we created to allow local startups to build awareness of their brand, and have the younger generation be exposed to innovations around them.
