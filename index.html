Who We Are

About Team QuadX 6299

We are Viperbots Team QuadX 6299 from Vandegrift High School in Austin Texas. We compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge and create robots yearly. We are a student-led team that strives to push the boundaries of innovation and learn as much as possible. We also do outreach to interact with the engineering community. This involves participating in company tours and hosting our own events to teach the younger generation.
